# About Ask China

**Ask China** is the world's first open Q&A community dedicated to China. We are committed to breaking down information barriers and helping people around the world discover the China they never knew through questions and answers.

## Our Mission

In an era of overwhelming information, discussions about China are often filled with bias and misunderstanding. Ask China's mission is to:

- **Build Bridges**: Connect questioners and answerers from China and around the globe
- **Deliver Truth**: Provide diverse, authentic, and in-depth perspectives on China
- **Foster Understanding**: Eliminate stereotypes and enhance cultural awareness through direct dialogue
- **Share Knowledge**: Build an open, permanently preserved high-quality knowledge base

Whether you're a traveler planning to visit China, a scholar studying China, a business person working with China, or simply a curious explorer, Ask China is your best platform for obtaining authentic information.
