# Ask China Cookie Policy

**Effective Date**: December 5, 2025

## What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites. They help websites function properly, improve user experience, and provide analytical information.

## How We Use Cookies

Ask China uses cookies for the following purposes:

- **Essential Operations**: To enable core site functionality like login and content posting
- **Performance & Analytics**: To understand how visitors use our site and improve performance
- **Preferences**: To remember your settings and preferences for future visits

## Types of Cookies We Use

### Necessary Cookies
These cookies are essential for the website to function properly. They enable basic features like page navigation and account access. You cannot opt-out of these cookies.

### Analytics Cookies
We use analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our content, which helps us improve the user experience. These cookies collect information anonymously.

### Functionality Cookies
These cookies remember your preferences and choices to provide enhanced, personalized features.

## Your Cookie Choices

You have the right to accept or reject cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can modify your browser settings to decline cookies if you prefer. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of Ask China.

To manage your cookie preferences:
- **Browser Settings**: Adjust your browser's privacy and security settings
- **Opt-Out Tools**: Use industry-standard opt-out tools for analytics cookies

## Third-Party Cookies

Some features on Ask China may use third-party services that set their own cookies, such as analytics providers. We do not control these cookies and recommend reviewing the third parties' cookie policies.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new effective date. Continued use of the site constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about our use of cookies, please contact us through the methods provided on our website.
