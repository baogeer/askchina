# Ask China Privacy Policy

**Effective Date**: December 5, 2025

Ask China ("we," "us," or "our") values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information when you use our services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: Username and other details you provide during registration
- **User Content**: Questions, answers, comments, and other content you post
- **Feedback**: Suggestions, complaints, and other information you submit through our website features

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Browser type, IP address, device identifiers, etc.
- **Usage Data**: Access times, page views, click behavior, etc.
- **Cookies**: Small text files used to improve user experience and website functionality

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information only for the following purposes:
- To provide, maintain, and improve our website services
- To verify user identity and maintain community safety
- To analyze user behavior and optimize product experience
- To comply with applicable laws and regulations

## Information Sharing and Disclosure

We promise not to sell your personal information to third parties. However, we may share information in the following circumstances:

- **With your explicit consent**
- **Legal requirements**: Upon lawful request from judicial or government authorities
- **Service providers**: With technical service providers who assist our operations (e.g., hosting, analytics services)
- **Public safety**: To protect the personal or property safety of users or the public

**Important Note**: Questions and answers you post on Ask China are **public content** and can be viewed by all visitors. Please do not post personal sensitive information in public content.

## Data Storage and Security

- We use encryption technology to protect your data security
- Your account information will be retained for 90 days after account deletion
- Public content (Q&A, etc.) will be permanently preserved to benefit the community
- While we implement reasonable security measures, we cannot guarantee 100% data security

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- View, modify, or delete your account information
- Export your personal data
- Request account deletion
- Refuse the use of cookies (may affect some features)

To exercise these rights, please contact us through the contact methods provided on our website.

## Protection of Minors

We do not provide services to children under 13 years of age. If we discover user information of minors, we will immediately delete the relevant accounts.

## Policy Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. Major changes will be notified via website announcements. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
